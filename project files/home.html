Human Development Index

A Machine Learning Web Application using Flask

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical measure developed by the United Nations to evaluate the overall development of a country.


The prediction is based on four important indicators:

  • Life Expectancy at Birth
  • Expected Years of Schooling
  • Mean Years of Schooling
  • Gross National Income (GNI) Per Capita

This application uses a Machine Learning model to predict the Human Development Index based on the values entered by the user.
